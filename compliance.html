COMPLIANCE

Compliance Program

Last updated: February 4, 2026

1. Our Commitment to Compliance

Science Based Body is committed to operating in full compliance with all applicable federal, state, and local laws and regulations. Our comprehensive compliance program ensures that we maintain the highest standards of integrity, quality, and lawful business practices.

This page outlines our compliance framework, quality controls, and the measures we take to ensure responsible research chemical supply.

2. Regulatory Framework

2.1 Federal Compliance

We operate in compliance with:

  • Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FDCA): Our products are classified as research chemicals, not drugs, foods, or cosmetics
  • Controlled Substances Act (CSA): We do not sell any DEA-scheduled controlled substances
  • Federal Trade Commission (FTC) Act: Our marketing and advertising practices are truthful and non-deceptive
  • Consumer Protection Laws: We maintain transparent business practices

2.2 State Regulations

We monitor and comply with state-level regulations, including:

  • State-specific restrictions on certain compounds
  • Business licensing requirements
  • Consumer protection statutes
  • Age verification requirements

2.3 Industry Standards

We adhere to industry best practices for research chemical suppliers:

  • Clear "Research Use Only" labeling
  • Third-party testing and verification
  • Proper documentation and record-keeping
  • Responsible marketing practices

3. Quality Control Program

3.1 Product Testing

All products undergo rigorous quality testing:

  • Identity Testing: Verification that products match specifications
  • Purity Analysis: HPLC testing to confirm purity levels
  • Mass Spectrometry: Molecular weight confirmation
  • Contaminant Screening: Testing for heavy metals and microbial contamination

3.2 Certificate of Analysis (COA)

Every batch includes a comprehensive COA containing:

  • Product identification and batch/lot number
  • Testing methodology and results
  • Purity percentage
  • Date of manufacture and testing
  • Third-party laboratory verification

Learn more: COA Verification

3.3 Supplier Qualification

We maintain strict standards for our supply chain:

  • Vendor qualification and auditing
  • Raw material verification
  • Quality agreements with suppliers
  • Regular supplier performance reviews

4. Marketing Compliance

4.1 Prohibited Claims

We never make:

  • Medical, therapeutic, or health claims
  • Claims about treating, curing, or preventing diseases
  • Dosage recommendations for human or animal use
  • Efficacy claims for human application
  • Testimonials suggesting human consumption

4.2 Compliant Communications

All marketing materials are reviewed to ensure:

  • Research use context is maintained
  • Scientific accuracy in product descriptions
  • Clear disclaimers are prominently displayed
  • No implied therapeutic benefits

4.3 Advertising Standards

Our advertising complies with:

  • FTC truth-in-advertising requirements
  • Platform-specific advertising policies
  • Industry self-regulatory guidelines

5. Customer Compliance

5.1 Age Verification

We verify all customers are 21 years of age or older:

  • Age confirmation required at account creation
  • Verification at checkout
  • Additional verification for flagged orders

5.2 Intended Use Acknowledgment

All customers must acknowledge:

  • Products are for research use only
  • Products are not for human or animal consumption
  • Customer accepts responsibility for proper use
  • Agreement to Terms of Service and Research Use Policy

5.3 Compliance Checkboxes at Checkout

Every order requires confirmation of:

  1. Research purpose acknowledgment
  2. Age verification (21+)
  3. No human consumption agreement
  4. Responsibility acceptance for handling and storage
  5. Terms of Service acceptance

6. Record Keeping

6.1 Documentation Retention

We maintain comprehensive records:

  • Order Records: Minimum 7 years
  • Testing Records: Minimum 7 years
  • COA Archives: Available for the life of the batch + 5 years
  • Customer Compliance Acknowledgments: Duration of customer relationship + 7 years

6.2 Batch Traceability

Every product can be traced through:

  • Unique lot/batch numbers
  • Manufacturing date records
  • Testing documentation
  • Distribution records

6.3 Audit Trail

We maintain audit trails for:

  • All orders and transactions
  • Administrative actions
  • Compliance acknowledgments
  • Customer communications

7. Order Screening

7.1 Review Procedures

Orders are screened for:

  • Unusual quantities or patterns
  • Shipping to restricted jurisdictions
  • Compliance with age requirements
  • Fraud indicators

7.2 Suspicious Activity

We may refuse or cancel orders that indicate:

  • Potential misuse of products
  • Resale for unauthorized purposes
  • Shipping to known problem addresses
  • Patterns suggesting non-research use

7.3 Restricted Shipments

We do not ship to:

  • International destinations (currently US only)
  • States with specific product restrictions
  • Addresses flagged for compliance concerns

8. Employee Training

All team members receive training on:

  • Product compliance and research-use requirements
  • Proper handling of customer inquiries
  • Recognizing and reporting compliance concerns
  • Privacy and data protection
  • Anti-fraud procedures

9. Compliance Monitoring

9.1 Internal Reviews

We conduct regular internal compliance reviews of:

  • Marketing materials and website content
  • Customer communications
  • Order processing procedures
  • Documentation and record-keeping

9.2 Regulatory Monitoring

We actively monitor:

  • Changes in federal and state regulations
  • DEA scheduling actions
  • FDA guidance documents
  • Industry regulatory developments

9.3 Continuous Improvement

Our compliance program is regularly updated to reflect:

  • New regulatory requirements
  • Industry best practices
  • Lessons learned from compliance reviews
  • Customer feedback and concerns

10. Reporting Concerns

We encourage reporting of compliance concerns:

  • Email: sales@sbbpeptides.com
  • Reports may be made anonymously
  • No retaliation against good-faith reporters
  • All reports are investigated thoroughly

If you become aware of product misuse or compliance violations, please report them immediately.

11. Cooperation with Authorities

Science Based Body cooperates fully with law enforcement and regulatory agencies. We will:

  • Respond to valid legal process and subpoenas
  • Provide records and documentation as required by law
  • Report suspected illegal activity when appropriate
  • Assist investigations related to product misuse
