ACCESSIBILITY

Accessibility Statement

Last updated: February 4, 2026

1. Our Commitment

Science Based Body is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying relevant accessibility standards to ensure we provide equal access to all users.

2. Conformance Status

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA standards. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities and user-friendly for everyone.

While we strive to adhere to these guidelines, we acknowledge that some areas of our Site may still need improvement.

3. Accessibility Features

Our website includes the following accessibility features:

3.1 Navigation

  • Consistent navigation structure across all pages
  • Skip-to-content links for keyboard users
  • Keyboard-accessible menus and interactive elements
  • Clear focus indicators for keyboard navigation
  • Logical tab order throughout pages

3.2 Visual Design

  • Sufficient color contrast ratios for text and backgrounds
  • Text that can be resized up to 200% without loss of functionality
  • Content that does not rely solely on color to convey information
  • Responsive design that works across different screen sizes

3.3 Content

  • Descriptive alt text for images
  • Clear and descriptive link text
  • Properly structured headings
  • Semantic HTML markup
  • Form labels and error messages

3.4 Interactive Elements

  • Accessible form controls with clear labels
  • Error messages that identify issues clearly
  • ARIA labels where appropriate
  • Accessible buttons and controls

4. Assistive Technology Compatibility

Our Site is designed to be compatible with:

  • Screen readers (such as NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver, and TalkBack)
  • Screen magnification software
  • Speech recognition software
  • Keyboard-only navigation

5. Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some content may have limitations:

  • Some PDF documents (such as Certificates of Analysis) may not be fully accessible. Please contact us if you need this information in an alternative format.
  • Third-party content or applications may not meet the same accessibility standards.
  • Some older pages may be undergoing accessibility improvements.

We are actively working to address these limitations.

6. Browser and Device Compatibility

For the best accessible experience, we recommend using:

  • The latest versions of major browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Current operating systems with built-in accessibility features enabled
  • Up-to-date assistive technology software

7. Feedback and Contact

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our Site. If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:

  • Email: sales@sbbpeptides.com
  • Support: Contact Support

When contacting us about accessibility, please include:

  • The page URL where you encountered the issue
  • A description of the problem
  • The assistive technology you were using (if applicable)
  • Your contact information for follow-up

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 5 business days.

8. Alternative Access

If you are unable to access any content or complete any transaction on our Site due to accessibility barriers, please contact us and we will:

  • Provide the information or service in an alternative format
  • Assist you in completing your transaction
  • Work to address the accessibility issue

9. Continuous Improvement

We are committed to ongoing accessibility improvements through:

  • Regular accessibility audits and testing
  • Staff training on accessibility best practices
  • Incorporating accessibility into our development process
  • Monitoring and responding to user feedback
  • Staying current with accessibility standards and guidelines

10. Formal Complaints

If you believe we have not adequately addressed an accessibility concern, you may file a complaint through:

  • Our support team: sales@sbbpeptides.com
  • Management review request: sales@sbbpeptides.com

We take all accessibility complaints seriously and will work to resolve them promptly.
