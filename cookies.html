Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, remember your preferences, and provide information to site owners about how visitors use the site.

This Cookie Policy explains how Science Based Body ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on sciencebasedbody.com ("Site").

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Site to function and cannot be switched off. They include:

Cookie Purpose Duration
session_id Maintains your session while browsing Session
cart_token Remembers items in your shopping cart 30 days
auth_token Keeps you logged in to your account 24 hours
csrf_token Security protection against cross-site attacks Session
cookie_consent Remembers your cookie preferences 1 year

2.2 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our Site:

Cookie Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics - distinguishes unique users 2 years
_gid Google Analytics - distinguishes users 24 hours
_gat Google Analytics - throttles request rate 1 minute

2.3 Preference Cookies

These cookies remember your settings and preferences:

Cookie Purpose Duration
currency_pref Remembers your currency selection 1 year
recently_viewed Tracks products you've viewed 30 days

3. Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use similar technologies:

  • Local Storage: Stores data in your browser for site functionality
  • Session Storage: Temporary storage that clears when you close your browser
  • Pixel Tags: Small images that help us track email engagement and site visits

4. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Enable essential site functionality (shopping cart, checkout, account access)
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how visitors use our Site
  • Improve site performance and user experience
  • Maintain site security
  • Remember items in your cart if you leave and return

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics (Privacy Policy)
  • Payment Processors: Secure payment handling
  • CDN Providers: Content delivery optimization

We do not control third-party cookies. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

6. Managing Cookies

6.1 Browser Settings

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View cookies stored on your device
  • Delete some or all cookies
  • Block cookies from specific or all websites
  • Receive alerts before cookies are stored

6.2 Browser-Specific Instructions

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

6.3 Impact of Disabling Cookies

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect your experience on our Site:

  • Essential cookies: You may not be able to use shopping cart or checkout
  • Preference cookies: Your settings won't be remembered between visits
  • Analytics cookies: We won't be able to improve based on usage patterns

7. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our Site, you will see a cookie consent banner allowing you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Customize your preferences
  • Accept only essential cookies

You can change your preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting our Site.

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when this policy was last revised.
